Classroom Redesign

TIL's Classroom Redesign initiative is an ongoing renovation project involving classroom spaces on the UofT campus. As student and faculty demographics continue to evolve, new technology and emerging teaching practices are changing the scope of what a successful instructional space looks like. In response, TIL's Classroom Redesign project continues to evolve and construct classrooms that support a diverse range of program offerings, while accounting for the diverse needs of classroom users.
