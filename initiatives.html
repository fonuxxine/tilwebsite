TIL Winter 2021 Teaching Pilot

The transition to online learning highlighted challenges with teaching in a new and unfamiliar environment. The pilot supported instructors to experiment with new teaching technologies and modalities from on campus during the pandemic to enhance the online learning experience for students. Learn more in the TIL Winter Pilot Report .

Learning Space Rating System

The Learning Space Rating System (LSRS) project provides a set of criteria to measure how well classroom design supports and enables active learning. This is based on Educause’s Learning Space Rating System .

Tech2U

The Tech2U program humanizes classroom technical support to foster excellent and innovative teaching and learning in an increasingly technological classroom. The program provides real-time, personalized technological support for instructors through a technical support team, moving from a room-based approach to one focused on instructor needs. Learn more about the launch of the program from the UofT News article .
